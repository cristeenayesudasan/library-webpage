Roll of the Dice

Author: Anand Neelakantan

Genre: Mythological Fiction

Summary:*Roll of the Dice* is a gripping reimagining of the Mahabharata, seen from the perspective of Duryodhana. The novel challenges traditional narratives and delves deep into themes of power, loyalty, and the human psyche. With its vivid storytelling and layered characters, it offers a fresh look at one of India's greatest epics.
