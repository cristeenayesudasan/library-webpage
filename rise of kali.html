Rise of Kali

Author: Anand Neelakantan

Genre: Mythological Fiction

Summary:Written by Anand Neelakantan, this novel offers a compelling retelling of the Mahabharata from the perspective of Duryodhana, portrayed as a misunderstood hero rather than a villain. The book delves into themes of social justice, caste discrimination, and the flaws of traditional dharma. Through vivid storytelling, it humanizes characters like Karna, Aswathama, and Eklavya, highlighting their loyalty and struggles. The narrative revisits key events, such as Draupadi's disrobing and the Kurukshetra war, presenting them through a fresh lens. Duryodhana emerges as a principled leader advocating for equality, contrasting with the Pandavas, whose actions are re-examined critically.
