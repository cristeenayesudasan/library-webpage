The Alchemist

Author: Paulo Coelho

Genre: Fiction

Summary: Written by Paulo Coelho, The Alchemist is a philosophical novel that follows Santiago, a shepherd boy from Spain, on his journey to find a treasure buried near the Egyptian pyramids. Along the way, he meets various characters, including a gypsy fortune-teller, a crystal merchant, and an enigmatic alchemist. Each encounter imparts wisdom about following one's dreams, overcoming obstacles, and listening to the language of the world. The book emphasizes themes of self-discovery, destiny, and the pursuit of personal legends. Santiago learns that true treasure lies not in material riches but in the journey itself and the lessons gained through it.
